About LocalEats

Discover the story behind Melbourne's premier culinary guide and the passionate team that brings you the city's finest dining experiences

Our Story

LocalEats was born from a simple passion: the love of discovering exceptional food and sharing those experiences with fellow food enthusiasts. Founded in 2020 in the heart of Melbourne, we began as a small team of culinary explorers who believed that great food brings people together and creates lasting memories.

What started as weekend adventures through Melbourne's laneways and hidden gems has evolved into the city's most trusted culinary guide. We've walked countless streets, tasted thousands of dishes, and built relationships with the incredible chefs, restaurateurs, and food artisans who make Melbourne's dining scene truly world-class.

"We don't just review restaurants – we curate experiences. Every venue in our guide has been personally visited, every dish carefully considered, and every recommendation made with genuine passion for exceptional food and hospitality."

— The LocalEats Team

Today, LocalEats serves thousands of food lovers, from Melbourne locals seeking their next favorite spot to international visitors wanting to experience the best of our city's culinary landscape. We're proud to be part of Melbourne's vibrant food community and to help connect people with the experiences that make dining in this city so special.

Our Values

Passion

We're genuinely passionate about food and the stories behind every dish. This enthusiasm drives everything we do, from our careful curation to our detailed recommendations.

Quality

We maintain the highest standards in our selections. Every restaurant, bar, and venue featured has been personally vetted to ensure exceptional quality and experience.

Community

We believe in supporting local businesses and fostering connections within Melbourne's food community. We're proud to champion the talented people who make our city delicious.

Discovery

We're always exploring, always discovering. From hidden laneway gems to established institutions, we help you uncover the full spectrum of Melbourne's dining scene.

Authenticity

We provide honest, authentic recommendations based on real experiences. No paid placements, no bias – just genuine insights from fellow food lovers.

Accessibility

Great food should be accessible to everyone. We feature venues across all price points and dietary requirements, ensuring everyone can find their perfect meal.
