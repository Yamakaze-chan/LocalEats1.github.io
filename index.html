NOMA D Melbourne

Tucked beneath the bustling Flinders Lane, NOMAD Melbourne offers a dining experience where fire and smoke meet the vibrant flavors of Spain, Morocco, and the Middle East.

The open kitchen and roaring wood-fired oven set the stage for dishes like smoked beef cheek with black garlic, wood-grilled cuttlefish, and their signature flatbread with za'atar. With a commitment to local produce and sustainable practices, NOMAD delivers bold, seasonal flavors in a stylish, subterranean setting.

Farmer's Daughters

Nestled in Melbourne's 80 Collins Precinct, Farmer's Daughters is a three-level culinary homage to Gippsland's rich produce, curated by Executive Chef Alejandro Saravia.

The ground-floor deli offers casual dining with Gippsland wines and beers, while the second-floor restaurant presents a formal farm-to-table experience, featuring dishes cooked over an open campfire kitchen. The rooftop bar provides an open-air oasis with locally sourced craft beverages. Each level immerses diners in the flavors and stories of regional Victoria, celebrating the connection between the city and the countryside.

Reserve a Table

The George on Collins

Beneath the historic Georges building on Collins Street lies The George on Collins—a stylish, New York-inspired basement venue blending Southeast Asian flavors with Melbourne's vibrant nightlife.

Known for its moody interiors and private booths, it's perfect for intimate dinners or lively gatherings. The menu offers a range of shareable dishes, from pork bao buns to sambal chicken wings, complemented by an extensive cocktail list. With events like bottomless brunches and live DJ sets, The George seamlessly transitions from a dining hotspot to a late-night destination.

Reserve a Table

La Cucina Melbourne

Tucked away on Little Bourke Street, La Cucina Melbourne brings the warmth of Italian hospitality to the heart of the CBD.

This inviting eatery offers a mouthwatering menu featuring hand-stretched pizzas, creamy pastas, succulent steaks, and fresh seafood. Complemented by an extensive selection of wines and beers, the restaurant provides a cozy ambiance perfect for both casual lunches and romantic dinners. Whether you're indulging in traditional favorites or exploring new culinary delights, La Cucina promises a delightful dining experience that captures the essence of Italy.

Reserve a Table

Supernormal

Nestled on Flinders Lane, Supernormal is a vibrant, all-day dining venue that seamlessly blends Japanese, Chinese, and Korean culinary traditions.

The menu features standout dishes like the famed New England lobster roll, succulent Szechuan-peppered brisket, and the indulgent peanut butter parfait. The minimalist interior, highlighted by a long open kitchen and neon accents, creates an inviting atmosphere. Whether you're in for a casual lunch or a lively dinner, Supernormal offers a dynamic dining experience in the heart of Melbourne's CBD.
